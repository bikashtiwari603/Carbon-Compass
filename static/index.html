Understand Your Impact 🌍

Explore how daily habits shape our planet's future

Transport Footprint Transport accounts for 24% of global emissions. Petrol cars emit 0.21kg CO₂/km, while metros produce just 0.04kg! Commute smart to save.
Home Energy India's grid emits 0.82kg CO₂ per kWh due to coal power. Simple habits like switching to LEDs and using 5-star ACs at 24°C slash your bills and footprint.
Diet & Food Meat diets produce up to 27kg CO₂/kg (beef). Opting for vegetables and composting organic wastes can save over 400kg CO₂ every year per person!
